Shelve

What is Shelve?


Shelve is a community book lending and borrowing platform that enables easy sharing of books within apartments, colleges, or local communities. Often we find ourselves craving a good read, easily accessible or we notice a booksehlf of lifeless books, collecting dust. Shelve creates a new cycle of life for books and through this, brings the community closer.

How to Use Shelve?


Add a Book

Go to the Add page and enter the book title, author, genre, and owner details.


Browse the Collection

Visit the Browse page to see all books currently listed in the community. Filter by genre or search for a particular author or book.


Borrow a Book

If a book is available, click Interested. Contact the owner through WhatsApp to discuss lending conditions and arrange a time and place for the exchange. Once the book is lent out, the owner should mark it as Borrowed so other users know it is currently unavailable.


Return a Book

Once returned, the owner must mark it as Available again so the next reader can borrow it.

FAQs


How do I lend my book?

Click on the Add section and fill in the book title. Click on "Find Book" and select the correct book. The rest of the details autofill.

After adding a book, if an interested person requests to borrow, chat with them on WhatsApp and decide on an agreed time and place to collect the book.

On the app, change the status of the book to "Borrowed" and once it is returned mark it back as "Available".


Can I trust another person with my book?

Shelve is built around communities of readers who share a common interest in books. While users are expected to treat borrowed books responsibly, any issues regarding damage or loss should be resolved directly between the lender and borrower.
